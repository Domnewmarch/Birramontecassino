History

Many quotations report that between the fifth and sixth centuries barley was grown for beer production at the Montecassino Abbey estates: legend has it that the ingredients of the first abbey beer in Europe, and perhaps of Worldwide.

Today, after 1500 years, the precious nectar is reborn, a beer with an ancient flavor, the daughter of a centuries-old tradition, obtained by recovering the ancient farms within the Tenuta dell 'Albaneta, where today barley is grown and recipes are processed.
